This is a slightly different kind of post than my usual tech ramblings. This year I've signed up for three months of back-to-back swimming challenges to support two amazing Cancer charities.

I really enjoy swimming, it's my favourite kind of exercise. But I often find I need a goal to motivate myself, so why not try and raise some money for charity.

## Swim 10k for Cancer Research UK (May)

In May I'm doing the [Cancer Research UK Swim 10k Challenge](https://www.cancerresearchuk.org/get-involved/find-an-event/swim-10k-challenge-may). The goal is to swim 10km cumulatively throughout the month to raise money for cancer research.

[Donate to my Cancer Research UK page](https://fundraise.cancerresearchuk.org/page/jacobs-giving-page-262820)

## Macmillan Swim It (June & July)

In June and July I'm doing [Macmillan Swim It](https://swimit.macmillan.org.uk/) for the second time. The challenge is to swim 21 miles across the two months, the same distance as the English Channel. I did it for the first time last year and enjoyed it enough to sign up again. Macmillan raises money to support people living with cancer and their families.

[Donate to my Macmillan page](https://swimit.macmillan.org.uk/fundraising/jacobs-fundraising-page446)

If you feel able to donate to either cause that would be great and I would really appreciate it. Thanks!
